Presented Projects

Innovative projects presented by students at the DIGIMARK's Tech Show Case 2025.

B-Trust Web Development

B-Trust

AI-driven platform to automate and enhance loan eligibility assessments.

B-Trust reduces manual screening inefficiencies and eliminates bias by leveraging machine learning models trained on historical financial data. It analyzes income, credit score, job status, loan history, and more to produce fast, fair, and explainable results for financial institutions.
Nexa AI Assistant & Education

Nexa – Smart Educator & Receptionist

AI-powered robot designed to revolutionize hospitality and education through expressive interaction and adaptive learning.

Nexa greets visitors, manages appointments, and guides users through services. As an educator, it delivers personalized, real-time learning across subjects. Ideal for schools, offices, museums, and tech hubs.
RobiX AI & Cultural Education

RobiX – Cultural Chatbot

AI-powered assistant promoting Cameroonian heritage through interactive conversations and voice-based learning.

Offers real-time translations, folklore guidance, and personalized cultural education for schools, tourists, and families. Supports local entrepreneurship and combats language extinction.
LUXILEDS Electronics & Display Tech

3D Cubic LED Display

A visually captivating LED cube controlled by microcontrollers to simulate 3D motion using light animations.

Ideal for education, exhibitions, and creative tech projects. Uses multiplexing and persistence of vision to animate LED layers. Great for learning electronics and hardware design.

TryOn Space Ecommerce Platform

TryOn Space

E-commerce platform that sells house furniture, clothes and accessories.

The platform has Augmented Reality integrated to permit users try different articles they might want to purchase before actually buying it.
DAT Timetable Automation

Digimark Automatic Timetable

DAT is a solution designed to manage scheduling by eliminating the inefficiencies of manual timetable generation-a process that is often time-consuming, prone to errors, and leads to scheduling conflicts.

DigiConnect Communication

DigiConnect

Digi-Connect is a web-based school management and communication system that helps universities and schools in Cameroon handle their daily academic and administrative activities in one place.

Smart Attendance Attendace

Smart Attendance

Smart Attendance is a web-based system that uses real-time image and video analysis to automatically record attendance, making the process faster, accurate, and secure.

Local Cloud Server Cloud

Local Cloud Server

Local Cloud Server is a self-hosted offline cloud system that provides secure file sharing, collaboration, and app hosting over a local network. It ensures privacy, low cost, and reliability without depending on the public internet.

SMARTID (S.I.D) Education & Identity Tech

Student ID & Attestation System

Fast, reliable platform for generating student identification cards and official attestations within academic institutions.

SMARTID streamlines the creation of student IDs and documents confirming enrollment, specialty, and academic status. Designed for schools and universities, it ensures authenticity and efficiency in student record management.

Quick Ride Mobile Development

Quick Ride

Ride-hailing platform connecting users to drivers for convenient travel.

Users can easily request rides, track drivers in real-time, and enjoy a reliable commuting experience. Designed with user-friendly interfaces and safety-first policies, Quick Ride supports both urban and suburban mobility.

Our annual innovation challenge brings together the brightest minds from across Cameroon to solve real-world problems using cutting-edge technology. This year's theme focuses on sustainable development and digital transformation in emerging markets.
